RANSOMWARE

Ransomware is a type of malicious software that encrypts a victim's files, making them inaccessible. Cybercriminals behind ransomware attacks often demand a ransom payment in cryptocurrency to decrypt the files. These attacks can target individuals, businesses, and organizations, causing significant financial losses and disruption.

  • 2024
    APRIL

    NHS London Ransomware Attack

    The National Health Service in London was hit by a significant ransomware attack, disrupting operations and compromising the data of nearly 1 million patients.

  • 2024
    SEPTEMBER

    Stillwater Mining Company Ransomware Attack

    The personal information of over 7,000 employees was stolen using the RansomHub RaaS. This highlights the growing threat posed by ransomware.
